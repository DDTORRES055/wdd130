Do I need experience to go rafting?

No, many rafting trips are suitable for beginners. Guides give safety instructions and help you throughout the trip

How long does a rafting trip usually last?

It depends on the river level and package. Trips can last from 1 hour to a full day, or even multiple days for advanced routes.
